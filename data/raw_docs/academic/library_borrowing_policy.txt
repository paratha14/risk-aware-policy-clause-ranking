LIBRARY BORROWING AND RESOURCE USE POLICY

1. Loan Periods
Standard loan items may be borrowed for 3 weeks and renewed twice online.
Items recalled by another user must be returned within 5 working days of
notification. Failure to return recalled items results in suspension of
borrowing privileges.

2. Overdue Fines
Overdue fines accrue daily. Outstanding fines above a threshold result in
suspension of library and IT services. Unpaid fines may be referred to the
finance department and added to student accounts.

3. Damage and Loss
Students are responsible for all items borrowed. Damaged or lost items must
be reported immediately. Students will be charged the full replacement cost
plus a processing fee. Deliberate damage is a disciplinary offence.

4. Special Collections
Special collections and archive materials may only be used in designated areas
under supervision. Removal of special collection items from the designated area
is prohibited and constitutes theft subject to disciplinary and legal action.

5. Academic Integrity
Photocopying beyond the permitted fair use limits and sharing library access
credentials are prohibited and may constitute copyright infringement.
