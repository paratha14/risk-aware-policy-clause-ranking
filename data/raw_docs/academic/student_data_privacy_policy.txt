STUDENT DATA PRIVACY AND RECORDS POLICY

1. Data Collection
The university collects personal data from students for purposes of admission,
enrollment, assessment, and student welfare. Collection of data beyond these stated
purposes requires explicit student consent.

2. Access to Records
Students have the right to access their personal academic records upon request.
Requests must be processed within 30 days. Denial of access without lawful basis
constitutes a breach of data protection legislation.

3. Transcript Release
Official transcripts are released only upon written student request or pursuant
to a court order. Release of transcripts to third parties without student consent
is prohibited except where required by law.

4. Data Retention
Student records are retained for a period of 6 years after the end of the student
relationship. Records retained beyond this period without legal basis must be
deleted upon request.

5. Data Breach
Any breach of student personal data must be reported to the Data Protection Officer
within 24 hours of discovery. Failure to report a breach is a serious disciplinary
offence and may constitute a criminal offence under applicable data protection law.
