CLASSROOM AND LEARNING ENVIRONMENT POLICY

1. Respectful Conduct
Students must treat all members of the learning community with respect.
Disruptive, abusive, or threatening behaviour in class will result in removal
from the session and formal disciplinary action.

2. Recording Policy
Recording of lectures or seminars without explicit instructor permission is
prohibited. Unauthorised recordings that are shared publicly constitute a
serious breach of privacy and intellectual property rights.

3. Technology Use
Mobile phones must be silenced during lectures. Use of laptops or tablets is
permitted for academic purposes only. Repeated misuse of technology after
warning may result in prohibition from device use during class.

4. Attendance
Attendance at all scheduled teaching sessions is expected. Students with
attendance below the required threshold may be barred from assessments or
referred to the academic support team.

5. Academic Accommodations
Students requiring reasonable adjustments must register with the Disability
Services Office. Adjustments cannot be applied retroactively to completed
assessments under any circumstances.
