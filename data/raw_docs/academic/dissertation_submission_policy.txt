DISSERTATION AND THESIS SUBMISSION POLICY

1. Submission Requirements
Students must submit their dissertation by the published deadline. Late submission
without an approved extension results in a grade penalty of one grade boundary
per week of delay. Submission more than four weeks late without approval results
in automatic failure.

2. Originality Requirement
All dissertations must be the student's own original work. Plagiarism, contract
cheating, or submission of AI-generated content as original work will result in
automatic failure of the dissertation and referral to the Academic Misconduct
Committee.

3. Word Count
Students must adhere to the prescribed word count. Submissions exceeding the
limit by more than 10% may be penalised. Submissions below 80% of the minimum
word count will not be accepted for assessment.

4. Ethical Compliance
Dissertations involving human participants, sensitive data, or secondary data
must include evidence of ethics approval. Submission without required ethical
clearance will not be processed.

5. Viva Voce
Students may be required to defend their dissertation in an oral examination.
Failure to attend a scheduled viva without prior notice constitutes a fail for
the dissertation module.
