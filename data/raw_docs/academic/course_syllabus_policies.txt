COURSE POLICIES AND EXPECTATIONS

Grading Breakdown
  Midterm Examination:    25%
  Final Examination:      35%
  Assignments (×4):       25%
  Class Participation:    15%

Minimum Passing Grade: 50%. Students failing to achieve 50% must retake the
course and may not proceed to dependent courses until the requirement is met.

Academic Honesty
All submitted work must represent the student's own original effort. Unauthorized
use of AI tools, copying from other students, or submitting previously graded
work is a violation of the Academic Integrity Policy. First violation results
in a zero for the assessment. Second violation results in course failure.

Submission Policy
All assignments must be submitted through the official course portal before
11:59 PM on the due date. Emailed submissions are not accepted unless the
portal is unavailable, in which case email submission must be accompanied by
a support ticket reference number.

Communication
Students must check the course email account within 48 hours. Failure to
respond to official communications regarding academic standing may result in
administrative holds on enrollment and registration.

Withdrawal
Students wishing to withdraw must do so before the published withdrawal deadline.
Unofficial withdrawal after the deadline results in an automatic F grade recorded
on the permanent academic transcript.
