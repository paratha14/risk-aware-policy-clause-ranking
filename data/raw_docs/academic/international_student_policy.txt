INTERNATIONAL STUDENT COMPLIANCE POLICY

1. Visa Compliance
International students must maintain valid immigration status at all times.
Failure to comply with visa conditions may result in reporting to immigration
authorities and termination of enrollment.

2. Attendance Monitoring
International students are subject to mandatory attendance monitoring. Absence
below 70% of required contact hours triggers a mandatory welfare review and
may result in reporting to immigration authorities.

3. English Language Requirements
Students who do not meet minimum English proficiency standards after enrollment
may be required to undertake additional language support. Failure to meet minimum
standards after support may result in suspension from assessments.

4. Fee Payment
International tuition fees must be paid by the published deadlines. Non-payment
results in removal from course registration and may affect visa status.
Outstanding fees must be settled before any academic transcript is released.
