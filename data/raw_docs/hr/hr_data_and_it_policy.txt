EMPLOYEE IT AND DATA POLICY

1. Acceptable Use
Company IT systems are for business use only. Personal use beyond minimal
incidental use is prohibited. Accessing inappropriate, illegal, or offensive
content on company systems is a gross misconduct offence.

2. Password Security
Employees must not share login credentials under any circumstances. Sharing
passwords resulting in a security breach is treated as gross misconduct
subject to immediate dismissal.

3. Data Handling
Customer and company data must be handled in accordance with data protection
laws. Unauthorized disclosure, transfer, or loss of personal data is a serious
disciplinary offence and may constitute a criminal offence.

4. Monitoring
The company reserves the right to monitor use of company IT systems. Employees
have no expectation of privacy when using company equipment or networks.

5. Software Installation
Installation of unauthorized software on company devices is prohibited.
Violations may result in disciplinary action and the employee may be charged
for remediation costs.
