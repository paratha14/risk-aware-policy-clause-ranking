RECRUITMENT AND SELECTION POLICY

1. Equal Opportunity
All recruitment must comply with equal opportunity legislation. Discrimination
on grounds of age, sex, race, disability, religion, or sexual orientation in
any stage of recruitment is prohibited and may result in legal action against
the organisation and the individual responsible.

2. Job Advertising
All vacancies must be advertised through approved channels. Appointing candidates
without following the approved recruitment process constitutes a policy violation
subject to disciplinary action.

3. Pre-Employment Checks
All offers of employment are conditional on satisfactory completion of reference
checks, right-to-work verification, and where applicable, criminal record checks.
Offers made without completing required checks may be withdrawn.

4. Misrepresentation
Candidates who provide false information during recruitment will have offers
withdrawn or employment terminated if discovered post-hire. Legal action may
be pursued for serious misrepresentation.

5. Internal Applications
Internal applicants must inform their line manager before applying for internal
vacancies. Failure to disclose an internal application may result in withdrawal
of the candidacy.
