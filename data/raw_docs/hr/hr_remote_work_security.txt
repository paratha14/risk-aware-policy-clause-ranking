REMOTE WORKING SECURITY POLICY

1. Approved Equipment
Employees must only use company-approved devices for remote work. Use of personal
devices to access company systems without IT approval is prohibited and constitutes
a data security violation subject to disciplinary action.

2. Network Security
Employees must connect to company systems via the approved VPN at all times.
Accessing company data on unsecured public Wi-Fi networks is prohibited. Violations
that result in data breaches may result in dismissal and financial liability.

3. Physical Security
Employees must ensure that screens are not visible to unauthorised persons.
Printed documents containing company data must be securely destroyed and must
not be left accessible to household members.

4. Incident Reporting
Any suspected security incident including lost devices, phishing attempts, or
unauthorised access must be reported to IT Security within 1 hour of discovery.
Failure to report incidents promptly is a disciplinary offence.

5. Home Environment
The company reserves the right to conduct virtual security assessments of home
working environments. Employees must comply with reasonable security requirements
or risk losing remote working privileges.
