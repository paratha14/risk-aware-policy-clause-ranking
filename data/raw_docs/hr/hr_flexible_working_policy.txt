FLEXIBLE WORKING POLICY

1. Eligibility
All employees with 26 weeks continuous service have the statutory right to request
flexible working. The company will consider all requests in a reasonable manner.

2. Request Process
Requests must be submitted in writing and must explain the proposed working pattern
and the effect on the business. The company will respond within 3 months of receiving
a request. Failure to respond within the statutory timeframe entitles the employee
to make a tribunal complaint.

3. Grounds for Refusal
Requests may be refused on specific statutory grounds only. Refusal on grounds
related to a protected characteristic constitutes indirect discrimination.

4. Trial Periods
The company may offer a trial period for a proposed flexible working arrangement.
Failure to comply with agreed conditions during a trial period may result in
termination of the arrangement.

5. Appeal
Employees may appeal a refusal within 10 working days. The appeal outcome is
final within the internal process. Employees retain the right to bring a tribunal
claim where the process has not been followed correctly.
