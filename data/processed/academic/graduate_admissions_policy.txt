GRADUATE ADMISSIONS AND ENROLMENT POLICY

1. Entry Requirements
Applicants who do not meet published entry requirements will not be admitted.
Fraudulent or misrepresented qualifications will result in immediate withdrawal
of any offer and termination of enrolment if discovered post-admission.

2. Conditional Offers
Conditional offers lapse if conditions are not met by the published deadline.
Requests for extensions are considered only in exceptional documented circumstances.

3. Deferral Policy
Deferral of entry is permitted for a maximum of one academic year. A second
deferral request will not be granted and the applicant must reapply.

4. Enrolment
Students must complete enrolment by the published deadline. Failure to enrol
without notification results in automatic withdrawal of the place.

5. False Declaration
Providing false information on any application or enrolment document is grounds
for immediate withdrawal of admission and may result in legal action.
