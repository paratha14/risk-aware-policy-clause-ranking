EXAMINATION BOARD AND AWARD REGULATIONS

1. Board Authority
The Examination Board is the sole body authorised to confirm student results,
awards, and academic progression decisions. No individual member of staff may
override a Board decision.

2. Extenuating Circumstances
Claims for extenuating circumstances must be submitted before the assessment
or within 5 working days after. Late claims without documented justification
will not be considered by the Board.

3. Repeat Assessments
Students who fail a module are entitled to one further attempt at the failed
component. A second failure results in module failure with no further attempts
permitted. Students failing core modules may not progress to the next level.

4. Award Classification
Attempts to influence award classification through improper means including
correspondence with Board members constitute academic misconduct subject to
disciplinary action and potential withdrawal of the award.

5. Transcript Accuracy
Students must report any errors in their academic transcript within 30 days of
issue. Alterations to official transcripts by any party other than the Registry
constitute fraud and are subject to legal action.
