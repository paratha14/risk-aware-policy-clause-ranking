SCHOLARSHIP AND BURSARY TERMS AND CONDITIONS

1. Eligibility Maintenance
Scholarships are awarded subject to continued satisfactory academic performance.
Failure to maintain the required GPA or credit load will result in suspension
or withdrawal of the scholarship without prior notice.

2. Conduct Conditions
Scholarship recipients who are subject to a formal disciplinary sanction may have
their scholarship suspended pending the outcome. Recipients found guilty of academic
misconduct may have their scholarship permanently withdrawn.

3. Repayment
Scholarships withdrawn due to misconduct, misrepresentation, or voluntary withdrawal
from the programme may require repayment of funds already disbursed. The university
reserves the right to pursue legal action to recover scholarship funds.

4. Non-Transferable
Scholarships are personal to the recipient and cannot be transferred, deferred
beyond one academic year, or converted to cash. Requests to transfer scholarship
funds to another person will be refused.

5. False Application
Students who provide false information in scholarship applications will have the
award withdrawn and may be subject to academic misconduct proceedings and repayment
demands.
