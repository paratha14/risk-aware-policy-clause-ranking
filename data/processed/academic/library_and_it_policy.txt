LIBRARY AND IT ACCEPTABLE USE POLICY

1. IT Systems Access
University IT systems are provided for academic and administrative use only.
Unauthorized use, access to restricted areas, or attempts to compromise system
security are serious violations subject to disciplinary action and may result
in criminal prosecution under applicable computer misuse laws.

2. Software and Copyright
Installation of unlicensed software on university equipment is prohibited.
Downloading or distributing copyrighted materials without authorization
constitutes copyright infringement and subjects the student to legal liability.

3. Library Resources
Library materials must be returned by the due date. Overdue materials attract
fines. Theft or deliberate damage to library resources is a disciplinary
offence that may result in suspension of library privileges and financial
restitution.

4. Data and Privacy
Students must not access, share, or modify other users' data without authorization.
Unauthorized access to another person's files or accounts constitutes a serious
breach of privacy and university policy, subject to immediate disciplinary action.
