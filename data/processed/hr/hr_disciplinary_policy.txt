DISCIPLINARY POLICY

1. Progressive Discipline
Standard disciplinary process:
 Step 1: Verbal warning (documented in writing)
 Step 2: Written warning
 Step 3: Final written warning or suspension without pay (up to 5 days)
 Step 4: Termination of employment

2. Gross Misconduct
Gross misconduct results in immediate dismissal without notice or payment in
lieu. Examples include: assault, intoxication at work, fraud, deliberate
destruction of property, or serious confidentiality breaches.

3. Performance Management
Employees failing to meet defined performance targets after a documented
Performance Improvement Plan (PIP) of 60 days may be dismissed for
incapability.

4. Appeal Rights
Employees may appeal any disciplinary decision within 10 working days of
written notification. The HR Director's decision on appeal is final.
