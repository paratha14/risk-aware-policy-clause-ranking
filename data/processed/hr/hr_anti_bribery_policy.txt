ANTI-BRIBERY AND CORRUPTION POLICY

1. Prohibition
The company has a zero-tolerance approach to bribery and corruption. Offering,
promising, giving, requesting, or accepting a bribe in any form is strictly
prohibited and constitutes gross misconduct subject to immediate dismissal and
referral to law enforcement authorities.

2. Gifts and Hospitality
Gifts with a value exceeding 50 / $50 must be declared to the compliance team.
Acceptance of gifts from suppliers or clients above this threshold without
declaration constitutes a breach of this policy.

3. Facilitation Payments
Facilitation payments small payments made to officials to speed up routine
processes are prohibited regardless of local practice. Any demand for a
facilitation payment must be reported to the compliance team immediately.

4. Due Diligence
The company conducts due diligence on third parties before entering commercial
relationships. Employees who engage third parties without required due diligence
approval may be held personally liable for resulting regulatory penalties.

5. Reporting
Employees who become aware of actual or suspected bribery must report it
immediately via the confidential whistleblowing channel. Failure to report known
bribery may itself constitute a criminal offence.
