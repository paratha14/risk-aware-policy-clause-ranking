SOCIAL MEDIA POLICY

1. Personal Use
Employees may use personal social media outside working hours but must not post
content that brings the organisation into disrepute, discloses confidential
information, or constitutes harassment of colleagues, clients, or competitors.
Violations may result in disciplinary action up to and including dismissal.

2. Company Accounts
Only authorised employees may post on official company social media accounts.
Unauthorised posting on company accounts constitutes a serious breach of policy
subject to disciplinary action.

3. Confidentiality
Employees must never disclose commercially sensitive, confidential, or personal
data through social media. Such disclosures may constitute gross misconduct and
a breach of data protection law.

4. Monitoring
The company reserves the right to monitor publicly available social media content
of employees where there is reasonable cause related to conduct or performance.

5. During and After Employment
Obligations regarding confidentiality and non-disparagement on social media
continue after the termination of employment in accordance with any signed
confidentiality agreements.
