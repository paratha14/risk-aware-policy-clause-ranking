EXPENSES AND REIMBURSEMENT POLICY

1. Eligible Expenses
Only expenses that are wholly, exclusively, and necessarily incurred in the
performance of employment duties are reimbursable. Personal expenses will not
be reimbursed under any circumstances.

2. Approval Requirement
All expenses above 50 / $50 require prior manager approval. Submitting expenses
without prior approval may result in rejection and repeated violations will be
treated as a disciplinary matter.

3. Receipts
Original receipts must accompany all expense claims. Claims without receipts
will not be processed. Submission of falsified or altered receipts constitutes
fraud and is subject to immediate dismissal and potential criminal prosecution.

4. Travel
Business travel must use the most economical option unless operational reasons
require otherwise. First-class travel requires Director-level approval. Unauthorised
first-class claims will be reimbursed at standard class rates only.

5. Expense Fraud
Submitting false, duplicate, or inflated expense claims constitutes gross
misconduct and fraud. The organisation will seek recovery of fraudulently claimed
amounts and may pursue criminal prosecution.
